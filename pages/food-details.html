What Makes Biryani So Special?

Biryani is more than a recipe — it's an experience. A slow-cooked, layered labor of love that tells the story of its ingredients with every bite.

How It All Comes Together

First, the chicken is marinated — lovingly — in yogurt and spices. Meanwhile, onions are slowly fried to golden perfection. Basmati rice is cooked until just shy of done, waiting patiently for its moment in the spotlight.

Why You'll Love It

Perfect for festivals, family dinners, or impressing that one friend who "doesn't usually like spicy food." A one-pot meal that's rich, filling, and deeply satisfying.
